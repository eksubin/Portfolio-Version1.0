Abhram

Transfer data through your fingers

  Abhram is an android based data transfer software which will let you to transfer data through your finger. The application was inspired by "SPARSH" which was released by MIT Media Lab.  Abhram can transfer images from one android device to other just by your touch.It works based on Dropbox cloud.The data is temporarly stored in dropbox and retreved on the point of next touch on any android based devices. The user have two modes either to transfer data or to receive it.It requires a dropbox account
