Hygge(Augmented greeting card)

Normal greeting cards are not meant to hide secrets from everyone. Whoever have the card can read whatever written in it. Here we found the possibility of augmented reality to hide secrets in a greeting card , the person who get the card have to use our specialised android application to read the secret written in the card. This greeting card is been given during farewell of my seniors in Robotics interest group.
Technology : The Greeting cards contain a marker(The cartoon of a dancing girl) near to the greetings message. This is considered as an image marer and tracked in the video stream of the android application. When the application detects the marker it overlays an image of our secret message .
