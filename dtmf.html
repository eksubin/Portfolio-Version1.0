DTMF Based Extension box

controls the extension box with your mobile

Control appliances using DTMF Module.DTMF or duel tone media frequency decoder detects the DTMF tones and generates the binary sequence corresponding to the key pressed in a DTMF keyboard or a mobile keypad.These binary codes are send to arduino. Arduino switches the AC supply and controls the extension box
