Halflife (AR Powered Social Robot)

Halflife is a social robot that exist in both real life and augmented reality. Social robots are becomning an integrel system in our life and Robotics research. But such robots costs a lot of money because of the complex systems that exist in them. Here we are trying to create a social robot which have its upper body in augmented reality and other half in real life.
Technology : The upper body of robot which is interacting with humans is designed using Unity and integreated into a mobile robot like firebird. The mobile robot base will give essential mobility for Halflife and the other half will be generated by an android applicaiton when it finds a marker over it.
